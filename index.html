Who We Are

Thoughtful Landscaping is run by Cody Chaves and Grace Davis — two Pacific Northwest natives who share a genuine passion for working with plants, people, and the land. Cody studies Environmental Studies at the University of Oregon, and Grace is pursuing Human Services at Western Washington University, bringing a combined focus on ecology, community, and care to every job.

Available from June through August, we take on everything from weekly maintenance to larger projects. From new garden installations to overgrown yard recoveries, we approach every project with attention to detail and a commitment to doing the job right. We prioritize sustainable methods, native and climate-adapted plantings, and landscapes built to thrive through every season.

We take the time to understand each client's specific landscaping needs, working closely with you to deliver results that match your vision and property. We serve homeowners and properties in Sammamish, Issaquah, Redmond, Bellevue, and the surrounding Eastside communities.

Our Services

Lawn Care

Weekly and recurring lawn maintenance — mowing, edging, aeration, and fertilization to keep your yard looking its best year-round.

Garden Design

Custom garden plans built around your property, preferences, and the Pacific Northwest climate. We design spaces you'll actually enjoy.

Planting

Trees, shrubs, perennials, and ground covers selected for your site's light, soil, and drainage — installed with care for long-term growth.

Mulching

Mulch installation to conserve moisture, suppress weeds, and build healthier soil over time. We source quality materials and apply them right.

Fountain Repairs

Fountain and water feature repair including pump replacement, basin work, and ongoing maintenance to keep your features running clean.

Blackberry Removal

Full removal of invasive Himalayan and Evergreen blackberry — roots and all — to reclaim overgrown areas of your property.
